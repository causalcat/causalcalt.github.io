Adamantia Zampeli

 
 


I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Centro de Ciencias Matemáticas at the UNAM Campus in Morelia since September 2021.

My research is focused on classical and quantum gravity as well as the foundations of quantum theory. I am also interested in quantum information and philosophy of physics, particularly in the philosophy of space and time, causation and the structure of physical laws. I currently work on the consistent histories approach and the general boundary formulation of quantum theory to tackle problems related to local measurements in quantum field theory.
